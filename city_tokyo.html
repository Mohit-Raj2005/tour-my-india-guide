Tokyo: Where Future Meets Tradition

Experience the electric energy of Shibuya and the serenity of ancient shrines.

Suggested 4-Day Tokyo Itinerary

Day 1: Modern Icons
Witness the Shibuya Scramble Crossing, enjoy the view from Shibuya Sky, and explore the trendy streets of Harajuku (Takeshita Street).
Day 2: Culture & History
Start at the ancient Sensoji Temple and Nakamise-dori in Asakusa. In the afternoon, visit the serene Meiji Jingu Shrine and Yoyogi Park.
Day 3: Tech & Entertainment
Dive into the neon chaos of Akihabara Electric Town for anime and gaming. In the evening, explore the nightlife and robot-themed dining of Shinjuku.
Day 4: Art & Relaxation
Visit the digital wonders of teamLab Planets (or Borderless) and relax in the beautiful Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden.

**Transportation Tip:** Purchase a Suica or Pasmo card for easy travel on the dense subway system.
