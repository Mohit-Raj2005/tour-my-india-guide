Essential Heritage Travel Tips

Best Time to Visit Heritage Sites

The winter months (October to March) offer the most pleasant weather for exploring forts and temples, especially in North and Central India.

What to Pack for Cultural Tours

Comfortable walking shoes, a wide-brimmed hat, sunscreen, and modest clothing (especially when visiting active temples or mosques) are essential.

Photography & Drone Rules

Photography is often restricted inside certain temples or tombs. Drones are strictly banned at many UNESCO World Heritage sites. Always check official signage.
