5-Day Kedarkantha Itinerary

  1. Arrival in Sankri; drive to the base camp and acclimatize.
  2. Sankri to Juda ka Talab; gentle ascent through dense pine forests.
  3. Juda ka Talab to Kedarkantha Base Camp; prepare for summit day.
  4. Kedarkantha Base Camp to Kedarkantha Summit (12,500 ft) and descent to Hargaon.
  5. Hargaon to Sankri; final descent and departure.

**Difficulty:** Easy to Moderate | **Best Season:** Nov - Apr

Essential Packing Checklist

  • Warm layers (thermals, fleece jacket)
  • Waterproof and windproof shell jacket
  • Sturdy trekking boots (broken in!)
  • Headlamp or flashlight
  • First aid kit & personal medications
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, and cap
  • Reusable water bottles

Remember: Leave only footprints, take only memories.
